Final Project Proposal

Due November 14 at the beginning of class

Read the requirements for the the final project and its timeline.

For an reminder of what previous final projects for this course look like, see the projects from summer 2016.

Give some thought to the following:

  • Your project idea.
  • A description of the data you will be using.
  • Where the data is probably coming from.
  • Probable work you will have to do with the data to make it usable.
  • Other information and skills you will need to make the project a success.

Submitting your work

You don't need to submit anything for the proposal, but please be prepared to talk about your proposal in class.
